Trilinos relies on many Third Party Libraries (TPLs). The following is a list of compatible versions for the most commonly used TPLs.

### Trilinos 12.6

*   SuperLU: 4.3
*   HDF5: 1.8.9, 1.8.12, 1.8.16, 1.8.17
*   NetCDF: 4.3.2, 4.3.3.1, 4.4.0
*   PNetCDF: 1.6.1
*   zlib: 1.2.8
*   Boost: 1.55.0, 1.58.0
*   yaml-cpp: 0.5.3
